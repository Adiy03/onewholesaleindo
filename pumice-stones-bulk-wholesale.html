Pumice Stones

Natural, Bulk, Wholesale, Supplier

Pumice stone is a lightweight, porous volcanic rock widely used in agriculture, construction, beauty, and industrial applications. Its unique texture and high porosity make it versatile and highly valued across industries. Our Pumice stones products:

a. White Pumice Stones

Lightweight, soft, highly porous – ideal for planting media, water filtration, and lightweight construction.

b. Red Pumice Stones

Attractive natural color, durable – great for landscape decoration and growing media

c. Black Pumice (Pasmal) Stones

Denser and heavier – suitable for industrial needs, filtration, and premium decorative elements

Specifications:

  • 0 – 0.3 cm – Powder or sand
  • 0.5 – 2 cm – Small granules
  • 2 – 5 cm – Medium-sized gravel
  • 5 – 10 cm – Rough-cut stone
  • 10 – 30 cm – Irregular natural stone
