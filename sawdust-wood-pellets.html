Sawdust & Wood Pellets

a. Sawdust Block (HS Code 440130)

Sawdust Wood Blocks are made from 100% dried and compacted chopped wood, blended with fine dry wood chips to ensure consistent burning and a natural wood aroma.

They are ideal as fuel for boilers, wood stoves, or fireplaces, and also have additional uses such as animal bedding, gourmet mushroom cultivation, etc

b. Wood Pellets (HS Code 440131)

Wood Pellets are eco-friendly biomass fuels made from compressed sawdust. They are used for residential heating, power generation, and industrial needs such as ovens and dryers.

The pellets offer low carbon emissions, high calorific value, energy efficiency, and competitive pricing. They are easy to store and transport, and made from renewable sources.

Wood Pellets Specifications:

  • Diameter size: 6–10 mm
  • Ash content: ~0.98%
  • Calorific value: approximately 4,716 kcal/kg
  • Color: dark brown (dark chocolate)
