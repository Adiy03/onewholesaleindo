What is One Wholesale Indo ?

One Wholesale Indo is a gateway for exporting wholesale products from Indonesia. It simplifies the export process into three easy steps

What products are sold through One Wholesale Indo?

A wide range of Indonesian goods. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to contact us directly.

How can I ensure the quality before placing an order?

Our suppliers undergo strict quality control. For most products, a Certificate of Analysis (COA) or quality report is available. We highly recommend requesting samples before confirming bulk orders.

What are the shipping options and estimated lead times?

We offer air freight, sea freight, and international couriers (e.g., DHL, FedEx, UPS). Lead time ranges from 3–15 business days, depending on the product and shipping method. Buyers may use their own freight forwarder if preferred.

Do you provide after-sales support?

Yes, we offer after-sales assistance via WhatsApp, WeChat, or phone. We’re committed to resolving any concerns related to product quality, documentation, or delivery.

What is your policy for defective or disputed shipments?

In case of proven defects, we offer free replacement or refund, depending on the agreement.
