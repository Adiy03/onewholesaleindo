RBD Palm Olein Oil

HS Code: 151190 Palm oil & fractions, processed

RBD Palm Olein Oil is refined from high-quality crude palm oil through a process of Refining, Bleaching, and Deodorizing (RBD) to produce a clear, pure, and versatile cooking oil.

RBD Palm Olein Oil Widely used in food manufacturing, frying applications, household cooking, bakery, confectionery, and HORECA industries, it offers excellent stability at high temperatures, a long shelf life, and a clean taste with no strong odor.

Uses and Benefits of RBD Palm Olein Oil

Uses of RBD Palm Olein Oil:

a. Household & Commercial Frying:

Ideal for deep frying and pan frying due to its high smoke point and excellent oxidative stability, making it suitable for restaurants, fast-food chains, and home kitchens.

b. Food Manufacturing:

Used in the production of instant noodles, snacks, confectionery, bakery products, margarine, and processed foods that require stable and consistent oil quality.

c. HORECA & Catering Industry:

Provides clean taste, long-lasting performance, and cost efficiency for hotels, restaurants, cafes, and large-scale food service operations.

d. Edible Oil Blending:

Commonly blended with other vegetable oils to improve frying stability, flavor neutrality, and shelf life for various cooking oil products.

e. Industrial Applications:

Utilized in oleochemical processes, soap manufacturing, and as a base oil for certain cosmetic and personal care formulations.

Benefits of RBD Palm Olein Oil:

a. High Heat Stability:

Resistant to oxidation and thermal breakdown, with a high smoke point of 230°C – 235°C, ensuring clean frying performance and longer oil usage time.

b. Long Shelf Life:

Refining and deodorization provide excellent resistance against rancidity, maintaining freshness during storage.

c. Neutral Taste & Aroma:

Enhances food quality without altering flavor profiles, making it ideal for a wide range of culinary uses.

d. Rich in Natural Antioxidants:

Contains tocopherols and tocotrienols that support oxidative stability and provide nutritional benefits.

e. Cost-Effective & Efficient:

Provides excellent frying performance at competitive prices, offering high value for food producers and retailers. RBD Palm Olein Oil’s combination of stability, versatility, and cost-efficiency makes it a preferred edible oil for both industrial and household applications.
