HS Code: 151190 Palm oil & fractions, processed

RBD Palm Olein is the liquid fraction obtained from the fractionation of RBD Palm Oil (Refined, Bleached, and Deodorized Palm Oil).

Through a controlled cooling and separation process, the more liquid component of the oil is separated, resulting in a clear, golden, and stable product suitable for various culinary and industrial applications.

Renowned for its excellent frying stability, RBD Palm Olein is the preferred choice for use in cooking oils, deep-frying, margarine, and food processing.

Its high resistance to oxidation and long shelf life make it ideal for commercial and household kitchens alike.

It remains liquid at room temperature in tropical climates, unlike many other vegetable oils that solidify under similar conditions.

In addition to food applications, RBD Palm Olein is also used in the manufacture of instant noodles, snacks, and processed foods, where its neutral taste and golden appearance improve product quality and consistency.
