Buy Bulk Wholesale Agarwood Oud From Indonesia


Agarwood, also known as Oud, is a rare and aromatic resin-infused wood formed in the heartwood of Aquilaria trees native to Indonesia..

Widely used in perfumery, traditional medicine, and spiritual practices, Agarwood is crafted into chips, powder, and essential oil (Oud oil) to serve diverse global markets.

Its unique scent profile, rarity, and natural origin make Agarwood one of the world’s most valuable raw materials, sought after by industries and individuals looking for luxury and authenticity.

Agarwood Oud Specifications:

1. Agarwood Chips / Wood Pieces

Agarwood chips are natural wood fragments infused with resin. They are traditionally burned for their rich smoke and fragrance, often used in religious ceremonies, meditation, or as a luxury room scent.

The distinct aroma profile of each chip depends on its origin, resin content, and age, making them highly collectible and prized in the Middle Eastern and Asian markets. HS Code: 121190.

2. Agarwood Powder

Agarwood powder is produced by grinding Agarwood chips into fine particles. It is widely used in the production of incense, bakhoor, and traditional perfumes.

Powder form offers versatility, as it can be used for direct burning, blending, or as a raw material for oil distillation. HS Code: 1211.90

3. Oud Oil (Essential Oil)

Oud oil is one of the most expensive and luxurious essential oils in the world, extracted through steam distillation of Agarwood.

It is a cornerstone of high-end perfumery, valued for its long-lasting, complex scent that evolves uniquely on each individual’s skin.

Beyond perfumery, it also carries cultural and spiritual prestige, being used in ceremonies, rituals, and traditional healing. HS Code: 3301.29

4. Others (Derived Products) Agarwood

Beyond raw materials, Agarwood is also transformed into a variety of derivative products:

  • Incense Sticks / Bakhoor

    Blends of Agarwood powder and oils shaped into sticks or blocks, widely used in homes, religious centers, and cultural events.

  • Perfume Blends (Attar / Mukhalat)

    Concentrated perfumes combining Oud oil with other natural oils, popular in luxury markets.

  • Handicrafts & Beads

    Agarwood is carved into prayer beads, jewelry, and decorative crafts, combining beauty with fragrance.

  • Wellness & Medicinal Uses

    Agarwood is believed to have calming and therapeutic properties, often applied in aromatherapy or herbal remedies.
