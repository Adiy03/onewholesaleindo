Agarwood Bulk Wholesale for Sale


Agarwood, also known as Oud, is a precious aromatic wood infused with natural resin, formed within the heartwood of Aquilaria trees from Indonesia.

Valued for centuries, it is transformed into agarwood chips, powder, incense, beads, bracelets, and essential oil (Oud oil) for perfumery, wellness, spiritual use and others.

With its rare fragrance, purity, and authenticity, Agarwood remains one of the world’s most sought-after luxury raw materials, available for both retail and wholesale

Agarwood Uses:

Perfumery & Fragrance: Agarwood oil (Oud oil) is one of the most precious ingredients in luxury perfumes, valued for its deep, long-lasting, and unique aroma.

Jewelry & Accessories: Agarwood beads and bracelets are worn as natural aromatics, bringing both fragrance and spiritual symbolism.

Cosmetics & Wellness: Agarwood oil is used in soaps, massage oils, and skincare products for its soothing and aromatic properties.

Spiritual & Religious Use: The calming scent of Agarwood is often used during prayers, meditation, and ceremonies to enhance focus and inner peace.

Incense & Bakhoor: Agarwood chips, powder, and resin are burned as incense, widely used in spiritual rituals, meditation, and cultural traditions.
