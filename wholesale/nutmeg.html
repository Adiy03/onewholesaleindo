Whole Nutmeg

Whole nutmeg consists of dried, intact nutmeg seeds that retain their natural essential oils and aromatic compounds. This form offers a longer shelf life and superior flavor stability, making it ideal for bulk storage, grinding on demand, and premium applications.

In the food industry, whole nutmeg is widely used in spice blending, sauces, bakery products, meat processing, beverages, and culinary preparations where strong aroma and freshness are required.

Whole nutmeg is also utilized in non-food industries such as essential oil extraction, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and traditional medicine, where high oil content and natural purity are important.

Powder (Ground) Nutmeg

Powder (ground) nutmeg is produced by finely milling dried nutmeg seeds under controlled conditions to preserve aroma, color, and flavor consistency. It offers convenience and uniformity for large-scale manufacturing.

In the food industry, powder nutmeg is commonly used in bakery mixes, instant food products, seasonings, confectionery, dairy formulations, and beverage flavoring due to its ease of application and consistent taste profile.

Powder nutmeg is also applied in nutraceuticals, herbal products, and cosmetic formulations, where precise dosage and fine texture are required.
