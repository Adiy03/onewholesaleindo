HS Code: 080111 Desiccated Coconuts

Desiccated Coconut is a dried, finely grated kernel of mature coconuts (Cocos nucifera), carefully processed to retain its natural flavor, aroma, and high nutritional value.

Valued for its rich coconut taste, long shelf life, and versatility, desiccated coconut is available in different grades such as fine and medium cuts.

It is widely used in confectionery, bakery, desserts, curries, cereals, and health foods, making it a vital ingredient for both household consumption and industrial food processing worldwide.

Desiccated Coconuts Uses:

Bakery & Confectionery: Desiccated coconut is a popular ingredient in cakes, pastries, biscuits, cookies, muffins, and chocolates. It enhances flavor, adds natural sweetness, and provides a soft, chewy texture that makes baked goods more appealing.

Desserts & Sweets: Widely used in puddings, ice creams, candies, and traditional sweets around the world. Its natural coconut aroma and pleasant crunch make it an excellent topping or filling that elevates dessert creations.

Culinary Dishes: Frequently incorporated into curries, sauces, soups, and rice dishes. It enriches meals with creamy flavor, boosts nutritional value, and adds a tropical twist to both savory and sweet recipes.

Breakfast & Cereals: A healthy addition to granola, muesli, energy bars, and smoothies. It provides dietary fiber, healthy fats, and sustained energy, making it a nutritious choice for an active lifestyle.

Food Industry Applications: Extensively used as a filler, binder, and flavor enhancer in processed foods, bakery mixes, ready-to-eat snacks, and health products. Its versatility and long shelf life make it a preferred ingredient for large-scale food manufacturing.

Desiccated Coconuts Types:

High Fat – Fine Grade

A premium quality desiccated coconut made from the finely grated kernel of fresh, mature coconuts. With a high fat content (typically 60–65%), it delivers a rich coconut flavor, creamy mouthfeel, and natural aroma.

Its fine texture makes it ideal for confectionery, biscuits, cakes, cookies, chocolates, and as a coating for sweets.

High Fat – Medium Grade

Produced from carefully dried and grated mature coconut kernel with high fat content (around 60–65%).

The medium particle size gives a coarser texture, perfect for bakery, cereals, desserts, energy bars, and savory dishes where added crunch and flavor are desired.

It retains the authentic taste and aroma of fresh coconuts, making it highly versatile for both household and industrial food applications.

Low Fat

Made by mechanically pressing and partially defatting the coconut kernel before drying and grating.

With a reduced fat content (typically 45–50% or lower), this variant offers longer shelf life and lower calorie content while still maintaining natural coconut flavor.

Widely used in diet-specific food products, health foods, bakery mixes, and processed foods where lower fat is preferred without sacrificing quality.
