HS Code: 080111 Desiccated Coconuts

Desiccated Coconut is a finely grated, dried, and unsweetened form of coconut meat obtained from mature coconuts, primarily cultivated in tropical regions..

Desiccated coconut is made by carefully removing the brown shell and paring, shredding the white kernel, and drying it to preserve its natural flavor and aroma.

One of the primary uses of desiccated coconut is in the food industry, where it serves as an essential ingredient in confectionery, bakery, and dessert products.

Desiccated coconuts adds natural sweetness, texture, and a rich coconut flavor to cakes, biscuits, chocolates, and traditional dishes.

Desiccated coconut is also widely used in the production of snack bars, breakfast cereals, and health foods due to its high nutritional value.

It is a good source of dietary fiber, healthy fats, and essential minerals, making it a wholesome and energy-rich ingredient.

In addition to food applications, desiccated coconut is used in cosmetic and wellness industries for its moisturizing and nourishing properties, often incorporated into scrubs, soaps, and natural skincare products.

Indonesia is one of the world’s leading producers and exporters of desiccated coconut, offering consistent quality and abundant supply.

The country’s fertile tropical soil and year-round coconut cultivation ensure a steady production to meet global demand.
