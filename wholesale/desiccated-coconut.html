HS Code: 080111 Desiccated Coconuts

Desiccated Coconut is a finely processed, dehydrated coconut product known for its natural purity, rich aroma, and long shelf life.

Desiccated coconut is a dried, finely grated or shredded coconut product made from fresh, mature coconut meat.

Coconuts are harvested from coconut palms that thrive in tropical regions. Once matured, the white coconut kernel is extracted, washed, blanched, and carefully dried to achieve a clean, white appearance and long shelf life.

Desiccated coconut is available in various cut sizes, such as fine, medium, and coarse, to meet different industrial and culinary requirements.

One of the primary uses of desiccated coconut is in the food and beverage industry.

Widely used as an ingredient in bakery products, confectionery, cereals, snack bars, chocolates, and traditional desserts. Its natural sweetness, texture, and rich coconut flavor enhance both taste and visual appeal.

Low Fat Desiccated Coconut

Low fat desiccated coconut contains reduced coconut oil, making it suitable for bakery, cereals, and health-focused food products that require lower fat content and better shelf stability.

High Fat Desiccated Coconut

High fat desiccated coconut retains more natural oil, delivering a richer flavor and aroma, ideal for confectionery, desserts, and premium bakery applications.

Medium Grade Desiccated Coconut

Medium grade desiccated coconut offers balanced texture and is commonly used for toppings, fillings, and inclusions in baked goods and snack products.

Fine Grade Desiccated Coconut

Fine grade desiccated coconut has a smooth, uniform texture, suitable for doughs, batters, coatings, and applications requiring even distribution.
