Coconut Milk is a creamy, white liquid extracted from the grated kernel of mature coconuts, prized for its rich tropical flavor, smooth texture, and essential nutrients.

To enhance convenience and shelf life, it is also processed into Coconut Milk Powder

Valued for their versatility, both coconut milk and its powder are widely used in curries, soups, sauces, beverages, desserts, confectionery, and bakery products, making them indispensable ingredients for household cooking as well as large-scale food and beverage manufacturing worldwide.

Coconut Milk & Powder Uses:

Culinary Applications:

Coconut milk and coconut milk powder are widely used in curries, soups, sauces, and stews, adding a creamy texture and authentic coconut flavor that enriches both traditional and modern cuisines.

Bakery & Confectionery: They are key ingredients in cakes, pastries, cookies, ice creams, and chocolates, providing smoothness, tropical aroma, and natural richness that elevate baked and sweet products.

Beverages: Ideal for smoothies, coffee creamers, cocktails, and health drinks, serving as a dairy-free alternative with a rich, satisfying taste.

Desserts: Enhances puddings, custards, frozen treats, and traditional sweets with a creamy consistency and authentic flavor.

Instant Foods: Commonly included in ready-to-cook noodles, sauces, and meal kits, offering convenience, long shelf life, and easy preparation.

Dairy Alternative: Valued as a plant-based substitute for milk and cream, suitable for vegan, vegetarian, and lactose-intolerant diets.

Nutritional Products: Used in health foods, infant formulas, and supplements for its natural fats, essential minerals, and energy-boosting properties.

Coconut Milk & Powder Types:

Coconut Milk

  • High Fat Coconut Milk: Thick, creamy consistency with rich coconut flavor. Commonly used in curries, desserts, sauces, and premium food processing.
  • Low Fat / Light Coconut Milk: Lighter texture, reduced fat content, often preferred for beverages, soups, or health-conscious products.

Coconut Milk

  • High Fat Coconut Milk Powder: Offers full-bodied creaminess, authentic coconut taste, and is widely used in confectionery, bakery, and rich sauces.
  • Low Fat Coconut Milk Powder: Provides lighter flavor and fewer calories, often used in beverages, instant mixes, and diet-friendly products.
