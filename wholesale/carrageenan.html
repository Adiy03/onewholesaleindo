Carrageenan for Sale: Indonesia Wholesale Trade

HS Code: 130239 Carrageenan

Carrageenan is a natural hydrocolloid extracted from red seaweed, valued for its exceptional gelling, thickening, and stabilizing properties.

Widely used in the food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries, carrageenan functions as an emulsifier, thickener, and stabilizer in products such as dairy, plant-based milk, sauces, desserts, and toothpaste.

Its versatility, high purity, and plant-based origin make Carrageenan a sustainable and effective alternative to synthetic additives, supporting industries that seek natural and eco-friendly ingredients.

Uses of Carrageenan:

Food Industry:

Used as a natural stabilizer, thickener, and gelling agent in dairy products, plant-based milk, sauces, processed meats, and desserts. Kappa Carrageenan provides strong, firm gels, while Iota Carrageenan creates soft and elastic textures.

Pharmaceutical Industry:

Applied as an excipient, stabilizer, and emulsifier in drug formulations, as well as in capsules, gels, and medical suspensions.

Cosmetics and Personal Care:

Used in lotions, creams, and toothpaste for its smooth texture, stabilizing ability, and moisturizing properties that enhance skin hydration.

Pet Food Industry:

Acts as a binder and stabilizer in wet pet food, improving texture, water retention, and overall consistency.

Industrial Applications:

Employed in printing, paper coating, and other specialized processes requiring viscosity control and gel-forming characteristics.

Benefits of Carrageenan:

Versatile Functional Properties:

Provides excellent gelling, thickening, and stabilizing effects suitable for a wide range of applications.

Eco-Friendly and Sustainable:

Sourced from renewable red seaweed, making it a biodegradable and environmentally responsible material.

Safe and Food-Grade Quality:

Available in Refined, Food Grade, and Petfood Grade forms, all produced under strict quality control to ensure purity and safety.

Thermally Stable:

Maintains consistent performance across various temperature ranges in both hot and cold applications.

Plant-Based and Non-Allergenic:

Naturally free from animal products, gluten, and common allergens, suitable for vegan and vegetarian formulations.
