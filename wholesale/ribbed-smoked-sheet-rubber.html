Ribbed Smoked Sheet Rubber RSS Manufacturer & Supplier

HS Code: 400121 Smoked sheets of natural rubber

Ribbed Smoked Sheet Rubber (RSS) is a natural rubber product created through a controlled processing method that involves coagulating fresh latex and then carefully smoking the sheets in a drying chamber.

This smoking process enhances durability, produces the characteristic brown color, and ensures consistent elasticity and strength across each sheet.

Uses and Benefits of RSS (Ribbed Smoked Sheet Rubber)

RSS (Ribbed Smoked Sheet Rubber) is a natural rubber product produced through a controlled process of latex coagulation, rolling, and smoking, resulting in durable sheets with strong elasticity and consistent mechanical properties.

It is widely used across tire manufacturing, automotive components, footwear, rubber goods, industrial parts, engineering applications, conveyor belts, hoses, and molded rubber products.

The benefits of RSS include excellent tensile strength, high elasticity, natural durability, strong abrasion resistance, good processability, and cost efficiency for mass production industries.

RSS also offers stable performance under pressure, flexibility for both heavy-duty and general applications, and environmentally friendly characteristics as a biodegradable natural material.

Its standardized grading system—RSS1 to RSS5—allows manufacturers to select the appropriate grade based on purity, color, strength, and industrial requirements, making RSS a reliable and versatile raw material for diverse sectors.
