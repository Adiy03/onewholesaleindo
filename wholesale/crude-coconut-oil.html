Crude Coconut Oil (CCO) for Sale Indonesia Bulk Wholesale

HS Code: 151311 Coconut oil, crude

Crude Coconut Oil (CCO) is a natural vegetable oil produced from copra (dried coconut meat) through pressing and initial refining processes.

Known for its high content of saturated fatty acids, particularly lauric acid This product is widely used as a raw material in various industries, including soap, margarine, shortening, cosmetics, and oleochemicals.

Unlike Virgin Coconut Oil (VCO), which is extracted directly from fresh coconut meat without excessive heating and retains the natural coconut flavor and aroma

Crude Coconut Oil (CCO) Uses:

Soap & Detergent Production: CCO is widely used as a key ingredient in the manufacture of soaps and detergents, thanks to its high lauric acid content that produces excellent cleansing and foaming properties.

Food Industry: Crude Coconut Oil (CCO) serves as the raw material which, after undergoing refining, bleaching, and deodorizing (RBD), is transformed into a neutral, odorless, and colorless oil.

In this refined form (RBD Coconut Oil), it is widely used as cooking oil, margarine, shortening, and a versatile base for many processed food products.

Cosmetics & Personal Care: CCO is utilized in the production of lotions, creams, and hair care products, providing moisturizing and conditioning effects.

Oleochemicals: An essential raw material for producing fatty acids, fatty alcohols, surfactants, and other derivatives used in pharmaceuticals and industrial formulations.

Industrial Applications: CCO is also processed into biodiesel, lubricants, and other chemical products, making it a valuable commodity in the global energy and manufacturing sectors.
