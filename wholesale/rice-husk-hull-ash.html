Buy Bulk Wholesale Rice Husk Hull Ash from Indonesia

HS Code: 262190 Ash and residues, others

Rice Husk Hull Ash (RHA) is a silica-rich by-product obtained from the controlled thermal combustion of rice husks generated during rice milling operations.

When processed under regulated temperatures, rice husk ash exhibits high amorphous silica content, making it a valuable material for industrial and engineering applications.

Rice husk hull ash typically appears as a fine, lightweight powder with low bulk density and high specific surface area. Its chemical composition is dominated by silicon dioxide (SiO₂)

In the construction and civil engineering sector, rice husk hull ash is widely used as a supplementary cementitious material (SCM).

Due to its pozzolanic activity, RHA reacts with calcium hydroxide in cementitious systems to form additional calcium silicate hydrate (C–S–H), improving compressive strength, reducing permeability, and enhancing resistance to sulfate attack and alkali–silica reaction.

For agricultural applications, rice husk hull ash functions as a soil amendment and mineral additive.

Its high silica content improves plant structural integrity, enhances resistance to pests and disease, and contributes to improved soil aeration and moisture retention, particularly in acidic or degraded soils.

In industrial manufacturing, rice husk hull ash is utilized as a raw material or functional filler in the production of refractory bricks, ceramics, insulation materials, rubber compounds, and polymer composites.

Its thermal stability, low thermal conductivity, and abrasion resistance make it suitable for high-temperature and wear-resistant applications.

Supported by an extensive rice milling infrastructure and controlled processing methods, Indonesian RHA meets international demand for quality, sustainability, and supply reliability.
