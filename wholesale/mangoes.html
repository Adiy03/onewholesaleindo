HS Code: 080450 Mangoes

Mango is a tropical fruit that is native to South and Southeast Asia, widely recognized as the "King of Fruits" due to its rich flavor, aroma, and nutritional value.

Mangoes is known for its smooth, colorful skin that ranges from green to yellow, orange, and red, depending on the variety and ripeness.

Mango Uses:

Fresh Consumption: Ripe mangoes are enjoyed fresh for their juicy, sweet, and aromatic flesh. They are often eaten as a snack, dessert, or used in fruit salads

Juices and Smoothies: Mangoes are widely processed into juices, smoothies, and nectar due to their naturally rich and creamy texture, offering a refreshing tropical drink

Desserts and Sweets: Mango pulp is used in ice creams, puddings, yogurts, cakes, and jams, enhancing sweetness and flavor with its tropical essence

Savory Dishes: Green (unripe) mangoes are used in chutneys, pickles, and salads for their tangy and refreshing taste, common in Asian cuisines

Dried Mango: Mango slices are dried to make chewy, sweet snacks that retain their natural flavor and nutrients, ideal for export markets

Mango Puree: Processed mango puree is used as an ingredient in beverages, sauces, baby food, and desserts, offering year-round availability

Indonesian Mangoes for Sale:

Indonesia is one of the prominent mango-producing countries in Southeast Asia, known for its diverse varieties such as Arumanis (sweet and fragrant), Gedong Gincu (reddish-yellow and aromatic), and Manalagi (rich and creamy).

With fertile soil and a tropical climate, Indonesia produces high-quality mangoes with excellent taste and aroma, suitable for both local consumption and international export markets
