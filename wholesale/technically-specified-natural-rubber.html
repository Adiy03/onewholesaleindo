HS Code: 400122 Technically specified natural rubber

Technically Specified Natural Rubber TSNR, also known as block rubber, is produced by coagulating natural latex and processing it under strict technical specifications.

This versatile raw material is widely used in the automotive industry for tire production, as well as in conveyor belts, footwear, hoses, seals, gaskets, flooring, and various engineered rubber products, thanks to its strength, elasticity, and cost-effectiveness.
