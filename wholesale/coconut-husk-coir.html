Buy Bulk Wholesale Coconut Husk Coir from Indonesia

HS Code: 530500 Vegetable textile fibres, not spun, n.e.s.

Coco coir is a natural fiber product derived from the coconut husk, processed into multiple forms including coco coir soil (cocopeat or coir pith), coir fiber, coconut mulch, and coconut coir mats.

Known for its durability, water retention, and biodegradability, coco coir is one of the most versatile coconut-based materials.

It plays an essential role in organic gardening, horticulture, and industrial applications, offering sustainable alternatives to synthetic products.

Coco Coir Uses:

Agriculture & Horticulture: Coco coir is widely used as a growing medium for plants, including potting mixes, coco coir soil (cocopeat), and biodegradable coco coir pots. It helps retain moisture, improve aeration, and support healthy root growth.

Landscaping & Erosion Control: Coconut mulch and coir mats are applied for soil stabilization, slope protection, and moisture retention in landscaping and gardening projects.

Industrial & Household Applications: Coir fiber is used in furniture, mattresses, car seats, ropes, brushes, mats, and handicrafts. It is valued for its durability, natural strength, and eco-friendly properties.

Packaging & Automotive: Coco coir serves as a sustainable material for packaging, cushioning, and insulation in automotive and industrial products.
