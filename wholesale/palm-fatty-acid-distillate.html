HS Code: 38231920 Palm Fatty Acid Distillate

Palm Fatty Acid Distillate (PFAD) is a by-product obtained during the refining process of crude palm oil.

It is primarily composed of free fatty acids (mainly palmitic acid)

PFAD appears as a brownish semi-solid substance at room temperature and has a distinctive mild odor characteristic of palm derivatives.

Despite being a by-product, it holds significant industrial value due to its versatility and chemical composition.

One of the main uses of PFAD is in the production of soap, candles, and animal feed, where it serves as an economical and sustainable source of fatty acids.

In the oleochemical industry, PFAD is further processed to produce distilled fatty acids, biodiesel, and surfactants, making it an important raw material in the renewable energy and chemical sectors.

PFAD also finds applications in lubricants, cosmetics, and detergent formulations, thanks to its natural fatty content and biodegradability.

Indonesia stands as one of the world’s largest producers of PFAD, benefiting from its abundant palm oil plantations and advanced refining infrastructure.

The country exports high-quality PFAD globally, supporting industries that require consistent, sustainable, and cost-effective fatty acid sources.
