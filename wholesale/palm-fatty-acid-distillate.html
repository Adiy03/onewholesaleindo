PFAD Palm Oil Manufacture & Supplier

HS Code: 38231920 Palm Fatty Acid Distillate

Palm Fatty Acid Distillate (PFAD) is a versatile byproduct obtained during the refining process of crude palm oil, composed mainly of free fatty acids.

Widely used in the oleochemical, feed, and industrial sectors, it serves as an essential ingredient for soap manufacturing, biodiesel production, and animal feed formulations.

Uses of Palm Fatty Acid Distillate (PFAD):

a. Oleochemical Industry:

Used as a key raw material for producing soap, detergents, candles, surfactants, and other fatty-acid-based chemicals.

b. Animal Feed:

Serves as an energy-rich ingredient in animal feed formulations, improving digestibility and overall feed efficiency.

c. Biodiesel Production:

Acts as a cost-effective feedstock for biodiesel manufacturing due to its high free fatty acid (FFA) content.

d. Food Industry (Indirect Applications):

Used in the production of non-edible food-grade additives such as emulsifiers and stabilizers.

e. Chemical Industry:

Used in the production of lubricants, plasticizers, and various industrial chemicals derived from fatty acids.

Benefits of Palm Fatty Acid Distillate (PFAD):

a. Cost-Effective:

Provides a more economical alternative to refined palm oil derivatives, especially for biodiesel and oleochemical applications.

b. High Free Fatty Acid Content:

Ideal for processes requiring high FFA levels, improving conversion efficiency in biodiesel and surfactant manufacturing.

c. Versatile Industrial Use:

Compatible with a wide range of industries including energy, chemical processing, feed production, and oleochemicals.

d. Sustainable Palm By-Product:

Derived from palm refining, helping reduce waste and supporting circular economy practices.

e. Stable & Easy to Handle:

PFAD has good storage stability and is easy to transport in bulk for export markets.
