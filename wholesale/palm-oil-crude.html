HS Code: 151110 Palm Oil Crude

Crude Palm Oil (CPO) is a natural vegetable oil extracted from the flesh of the palm fruit through pressing and minimal refining processes.

CPO is valued for its rich carotene content and balanced composition of saturated and unsaturated fatty acids, making it a key raw material in the global palm oil industry.

Palm oil is recognized for its consistent quality and sustainable supply, supporting industries worldwide in applications such as cooking oil, soap making, margarine, shortening, oleochemicals, animal feed, and various other palm oil uses.

Its cost-effectiveness, long shelf life, and multifunctional properties make it an indispensable ingredient in the global supply chain.

Crude Palm Oil Uses:

Oleochemicals & Industrial Uses: CPO is processed into derivatives used in detergents, lubricants, candles, and biodiesel industries.

Soap & Cosmetics: Palm oil is a key ingredient in soap making, skincare, and cosmetic products due to its natural fatty acid content.

Cooking Oil & Food Industry: Crude Palm Oil is widely refined and processed into edible vegetable oil for frying, baking, and margarine production. It is valued for its stability at high temperatures and long shelf life.

Animal Feed & By-Products: Residues from crude palm oil processing are used as raw materials for palm kernel expeller cake, an important component in livestock feed.
