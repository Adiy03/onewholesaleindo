Palm Kernel Shell (PKS), sometimes referred to as palm shell, kernel shells, or palm kernel husk, is a natural by-product of the palm oil industry obtained after the extraction of palm kernel oil.

PKS is widely used as an alternative, renewable, and cost-effective biomass fuel.

With high calorific value and low ash content, palm kernel shells are considered an excellent option for power plants, boilers, and other industrial energy applications.

Palm Kernel Shell PKS Uses:

Biomass Fuel: Palm kernel shells are widely used as renewable biomass fuel for power plants, boilers, and industrial heating. They provide high calorific value and low ash content, making them a reliable alternative to coal.

Cement Industry: PKS is utilized as an energy source in cement factories, replacing fossil fuels and supporting cleaner production processes.

Gasification & Energy Production: Palm kernel shells are suitable for gasification technology, producing syngas that can be used for electricity and heat generation.
