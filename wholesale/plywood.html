Buy Bulk Wholesale Plywood from Indonesia

HS Code: 4412Xa Wood Plywood

Plywood is an engineered wood product made by bonding multiple layers of wood veneer together with strong adhesives.

Each layer is arranged with alternating grain directions to enhance strength, durability, and dimensional stability.

Plywood is widely used across construction, furniture manufacturing, interior design, packaging, and industrial applications due to its excellent structural performance and cost efficiency.

Types of Plywood

  • Commercial Plywood – Suitable for interior applications such as furniture, partitions, and wall panels. Cost-effective with reliable strength.
  • Marine Plywood – Manufactured using waterproof adhesives, making it ideal for high-moisture environments, shipbuilding, and exterior construction.
  • Film Faced Plywood – Coated with a phenolic film, commonly used for concrete formwork due to its smooth surface and reusability.
  • Hardwood Plywood – Made from hardwood veneers, offering high strength and durability for furniture and flooring applications.
  • Softwood Plywood – Commonly used in construction, roofing, and structural applications where flexibility and strength are required.

One of the main advantages of plywood is its high strength-to-weight ratio. It resists cracking, warping, and shrinkage better than solid wood, making it a reliable choice for long-term use in demanding environments.

Plywood can be produced with various thicknesses, sizes, glue types, and surface finishes to meet specific industry standards and project requirements. Custom specifications are available for bulk and export orders.

Indonesia is one of the leading producers of high-quality plywood, supported by sustainable forestry practices, skilled manufacturing processes, and international quality standards. Indonesian plywood is widely exported to global markets for residential, commercial, and industrial use.
