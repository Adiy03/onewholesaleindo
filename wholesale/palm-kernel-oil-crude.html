Crude Palm Kernel Oil for Sale: Indonesia Wholesale Trade

HS Code: 151321 Palm Kernel Oil, Crude

Crude Palm Kernel Oil (CPKO) is a natural vegetable oil extracted from the kernel, or seed, of the oil palm fruit which thrives in tropical regions.

The oil palm tree is highly valued for producing two distinct types of oil — Crude Palm Oil (CPO) from the fruit’s flesh and Crude Palm Kernel Oil (CPKO) from its seed. CPKO has a pale yellow color and a characteristic nutty aroma, distinguishing it from other palm-derived oils. One of the primary uses of Crude Palm Kernel Oil is as a raw material for the production of lauric-based oils and fats, widely utilized in the manufacturing of soaps, detergents, cosmetics, and personal care products. Its high lauric acid content makes it an essential ingredient in producing surfactants and other oleochemical derivatives. CPKO also plays a vital role in the food industry, where it serves as a component in margarine, confectionery fats, and bakery shortenings. Its natural stability and oxidative resistance make it suitable for use in various processed food applications. Beyond food and cosmetics, Crude Palm Kernel Oil is increasingly recognized as a sustainable feedstock for biodiesel production, contributing to renewable energy initiatives and reducing dependence on fossil fuels. Indonesia stands as one of the world’s leading producers and exporters of Crude Palm Kernel Oil, supported by vast palm plantations and advanced processing facilities. The country's favorable climate and sustainable cultivation practices ensure a consistent supply of high-quality CPKO to meet global market demand.
