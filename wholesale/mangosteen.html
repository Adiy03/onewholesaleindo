Mangosteen for Sale: Indonesia Bulk Wholesale Trade

HS Code: 080450 Mangosteen

Mangosteen is a tropical fruit native to Southeast Asia, often referred to as the “Queen of Fruits” due to its delicate balance of sweetness and acidity, along with its refreshing flavor and numerous health benefits

The fruit is round with a thick, purple rind and soft white segments inside, offering a juicy, aromatic taste that is both sweet and tangy

Mangosteen Uses:

Fresh Consumption: Ripe mangosteen is enjoyed fresh as a tropical delicacy. Its juicy and fragrant flesh makes it one of the most loved fruits in Asia and beyond

Juices and Beverages: Mangosteen juice and extract are widely used in health drinks for their refreshing flavor and antioxidant properties

Desserts: The sweet, aromatic pulp of mangosteen can be used in ice creams, sorbets, jams, and pastries, adding a unique tropical touch

Nutraceuticals: Mangosteen is rich in xanthones, powerful antioxidants known for their anti-inflammatory and immune-boosting effects, making it popular in health supplements and skincare products

Dried Mangosteen: The fruit can be dried or freeze-dried to make snack products or used as an ingredient in tea blends and health foods

Indonesian Mangosteens for Sale:

Indonesia is one of the major producers and exporters of mangosteen in the world, especially from regions such as West Java, Bali, and Sumatra. The country's tropical climate and fertile soil produce mangosteens with bright purple skins and high-quality, white-fleshed pulp

Indonesian mangosteens are highly valued in international markets for their superior sweetness, aroma, and texture. The fruit meets export-grade standards and is shipped to countries such as China, Thailand, the Middle East, and Europe
