HS Code: 080450 Mangosteen

Mangosteen is a premium tropical fruit native to Southeast Asia, widely known for its deep purple rind and soft, snow-white flesh with a delicately sweet and tangy flavor.

Often referred to as the “Queen of Fruits,” mangosteen is highly valued in international fresh fruit markets for its taste, appearance, and nutritional profile.

The mangosteen tree thrives in tropical climates and produces round fruits protected by a thick outer shell. Inside, the fruit contains segmented, juicy flesh with a smooth texture and refreshing aroma, making it a favorite for fresh consumption as well as for use in beverages, desserts, and processed fruit products.

Its unique flavor profile and premium image make it popular in high-end retail, hospitality, and food service industries. Mangosteen is also processed into juices, concentrates, frozen fruit, and dietary supplements.

Mangosteen is rich in antioxidants, particularly xanthones, along with vitamins and minerals that contribute to overall wellness.

These health benefits have increased global demand for mangosteen in functional food, nutraceutical, and health-focused product industries.

Indonesia is one of the world’s leading producers of mangosteen, supplying high-quality fruit to international markets. The country’s fertile soil, tropical climate, and established agricultural practices ensure consistent production, excellent fruit quality, and reliable export volumes.
