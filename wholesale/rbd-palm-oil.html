HS Code: 151190 Palm oil & fractions, processed

RBD Palm Oil (Refined, Bleached, and Deodorized Palm Oil) is a high-quality vegetable oil derived from the fruit of the oil palm tree, which thrives in tropical regions.

The oil palm tree is known for its abundant fruit bunches that contain oil-rich mesocarp, from which crude palm oil is extracted.

This crude oil undergoes a refining process — including refining, bleaching, and deodorizing — to produce RBD Palm Oil, a pure, light-colored, and odorless product suitable for various industrial and food applications.

One of the primary uses of RBD Palm Oil is in the food industry, where it serves as a key ingredient in cooking oils, margarine, shortening, and baked goods.

Its high stability, neutral flavor, and resistance to oxidation make it ideal for frying and food manufacturing.

RBD Palm Oil is also widely used in the non-food sector, including the production of soaps, detergents, cosmetics, and biofuels.

Its versatility and consistent quality make it a preferred choice for manufacturers across diverse industries.

In addition to its functional benefits, RBD Palm Oil is valued for its balanced fatty acid composition, containing both saturated and unsaturated fats, as well as natural antioxidants like tocopherols and tocotrienols (vitamin E), which contribute to its stability and shelf life.

Furthermore, RBD Palm Oil can be fractionated — a process of controlled cooling and separation — to obtain two main components:

RBD Palm Olein, the liquid fraction, which remains clear at room temperature and is widely used as a premium frying oil and cooking ingredient.

RBD Palm Stearin, the solid fraction, which is used in the production of margarine, shortening, bakery fats, soaps, candles, and other industrial products.

This fractionation process enhances the versatility of palm oil, allowing it to meet diverse market needs across both the food and non-food sectors.

Indonesia is one of the world’s largest producers and exporters of RBD Palm Oil. The country’s vast oil palm plantations, favorable tropical climate, and advanced processing facilities ensure a reliable supply of premium-quality RBD Palm Oil for global markets.
