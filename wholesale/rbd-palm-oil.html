RBD Palm Oil

HS Code: 151190 Palm oil & fractions, processed

RBD Palm Oil is produced from refined crude palm oil through a series of processes including bleaching and deodorizing.

RBD Palm Oil high quality, consistent purity, and cost-effective performance make RBD Palm Oil an essential raw material for industries seeking reliable, efficient, and sustainable solutions in both food and non-food production.

Furthermore, RBD Palm Oil can be fractionated — a process of controlled cooling and separation — to obtain two main components:

a. RBD Palm Olein Oil

The liquid fraction, which remains clear at room temperature and is widely used as a premium frying oil and cooking ingredient.

b. RBD Palm Stearin

The solid fraction, which is used in the production of margarine, shortening, bakery fats, soaps, candles, and other industrial products.

This fractionation process enhances the versatility of palm oil, allowing it to meet diverse market needs across both the food and non-food sectors.

Uses and Benefits of RBD Palm Oil

RBD Palm Oil (Refined, Bleached, and Deodorized Palm Oil) is a high-quality edible and industrial oil produced from crude palm oil through advanced refining processes that remove impurities, color, and odor. Its stable composition and neutral taste make it one of the most widely used vegetable oils across global industries.

Uses of RBD Palm Oil:

a. Food Industry & Cooking Applications:

Commonly used for frying, baking, and food processing due to its high stability, long shelf life, and resistance to oxidation. Widely used in margarine, shortening, confectionery, snacks, and instant foods.

b. Personal Care & Cosmetics:

Serves as a base ingredient in soaps, shampoos, lotions, creams, and skincare products thanks to its moisturizing properties and smooth texture.

c. Household & Cleaning Products:

Essential raw material for manufacturing soaps, detergents, and cleaning agents due to its excellent foam stability and cleansing performance.

d. Industrial Manufacturing:

Used in producing lubricants, candles, surfactants, and other industrial formulations that require a stable and cost-efficient vegetable-based oil.

e. Biodiesel Production:

RBD Palm Oil is an important feedstock for biodiesel manufacturing, supporting renewable energy initiatives and eco-friendly fuel development.

f. Pharmaceutical & Nutritional Products:

Applied in medicinal ointments, supplements, and vitamin formulations due to its natural fatty acid profile and safe food-grade quality.

Benefits of RBD Palm Oil:

a. High Oxidative Stability:

Its composition allows excellent performance in high-temperature applications, making it ideal for cooking and industrial processes.

b. Neutral Taste & Aroma:

Provides clean, consistent flavor suitable for food manufacturing without altering product characteristics.

c. Versatile Functional Properties:

Offers smooth texture, good consistency, and stable performance across food, cosmetic, and chemical industries.

d. Cost-Effective & Efficient:

Provides superior functionality at competitive prices, making it a preferred choice for large-scale manufacturers worldwide.

e. Supports Sustainable Production:

Often sourced from certified plantations, RBD Palm Oil contributes to environmentally responsible and renewable supply chains. RBD Palm Oil’s stability, versatility, and broad industry applications make it an essential raw material for companies seeking reliable, high-quality, and cost-efficient production inputs.
