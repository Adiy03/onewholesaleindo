HS Code: 151321 Palm Kernel Oil, Crude

Palm Kernel Oil is a natural vegetable oil extracted from the kernel or seed of the oil palm fruit

Palm kernel oil distinct from Palm Oil, which is derived from the fleshy part of the same fruit.

Palm kernel oil highly valued for its versatility, stability, and rich composition of lauric acid, making it an essential ingredient in various industries ranging from food to cosmetics and manufacturing.

While both come from the oil palm, Palm Kernel Oil has a higher concentration of lauric acid.

Palm Kernel Oil Uses:

Food Industry: Palm kernel oil is widely used in the production of margarine, shortening, and confectionery fats due to its semi-solid texture and high oxidative stability. It provides a creamy consistency and extends shelf life in processed foods.

Cosmetics and Personal Care: Thanks to its moisturizing and emollient properties, palm kernel oil is a key ingredient in soaps, lotions, and creams. It nourishes the skin and creates a smooth, gentle texture in skincare formulations.

Industrial Applications: Palm kernel oil and its derivatives are used in the production of surfactants, detergents, and lubricants. Its stable chemical structure makes it suitable for industrial processes requiring high performance and long-lasting results.

Biofuel Production: The oil’s high energy content and renewability make it a valuable raw material for biodiesel and renewable energy applications.
