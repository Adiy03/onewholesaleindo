Palm Kernel Expeller, also known as Palm Kernel Cake or Palm Kernel Meal, is a by-product of palm kernel oil extraction.

Palm kernel expeller cake meal is a highly valuable ingredient in the animal feed industry due to its excellent nutritional content and affordability.

PKE is produced after the mechanical pressing or solvent extraction of palm kernels to obtain palm kernel oil.

The remaining solid residue forms a coarse, brown meal rich in protein and energy, making it an ideal feed supplement for cattle, sheep, goats, and poultry.

Palm Kernel Expeller Uses:

Animal Feed: Widely used as a feed ingredient for ruminants, PKE enhances milk production and provides essential fiber and protein. It is often mixed with other feed sources such as corn or soy meal.

Organic Fertilizer: The nutrient-rich residue can be used as an organic fertilizer, helping improve soil health and crop productivity.

Biomass Energy: In some industries, PKE is also utilized as a biomass fuel due to its calorific value, contributing to renewable energy sources.
