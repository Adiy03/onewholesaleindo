HS Code:
230660 Oilcake of palm nuts or kernels

Palm Kernel Expeller (PKE), also known as Palm Kernel Cake (PKC), is a by-product of the palm kernel oil extraction process.

After the oil is extracted from the palm kernel seed, the remaining solid material rich in fiber and protein is referred to as Palm Kernel Expeller (Cake)

Palm Kernel Expeller (Cake) Uses:

Cattle Feed: High in digestible fiber and energy, widely used in dairy and beef rations to enhance milk yield and support healthy weight gain.

Sheep & Goat Feed: Provides a valuable source of energy and protein, improving rumen health and overall productivity in small ruminants.

Poultry Feed: Included in controlled amounts to supplement energy and protein in chicken feed formulations.

Aquaculture Feed: Can be processed into balanced fish and shrimp feed to supply fiber and nutrients.

Organic Fertilizer: Residual meal serves as a natural soil conditioner, improving fertility and sustainability in agriculture.

Biomass Fuel: An alternative renewable energy source, used as biomass material for industrial boilers and power generation.

Palm Kernel Expeller (Cake) Benefits:

Cost-Effective: A more affordable substitute for traditional feed ingredients like soybean meal and corn.

High Fiber Content: Contains a high level of crude fiber, which is good for ruminant digestion.

Plant-Based Protein Source: Contains moderate protein (14–18%), suitable for cattle, goats, and sheep feed.

Available Energy: Provides fat and carbohydrates as an additional energy source.

Eco-Friendly: A by-product of the palm oil industry, supporting the zero waste concept.

Indonesia Palm Kernel Expeller (PKE)

Indonesia is the world’s leading producer and exporter of palm oil, and also a major supplier of high-quality Palm Kernel Expeller (PKE).

PKE is a by-product of palm kernel oil extraction, presented in pellet or powder form with a brownish color, rich in fiber, and highly digestible energy.

Sourced sustainably from plantations in Sumatra, Kalimantan, and Sulawesi, Palm Kernel Expeller from Indonesia is widely used in the livestock sector, particularly as feed for dairy cows, beef cattle, sheep, and goats.

Its natural plant-based protein, crude fiber, and fat content help support growth, improve weight gain, and enhance milk production.

Key advantages of Indonesian PKE include:
  • Large & sustainable supply: supported by Indonesia’s massive palm oil industry.
  • Consistent quality: produced under international industry standards.
  • Competitive pricing: making it a cost-effective alternative compared to other feed sources.
  • Versatility: suitable for use as concentrate feed ingredients or as an additional energy source in feed rations.
