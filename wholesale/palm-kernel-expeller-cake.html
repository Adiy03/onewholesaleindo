Palm Kernel Expeller for Cattle Feed & Cows

HS Code: 230660 Of palm nuts or kernels

Palm Kernel Expeller (PKE) is produced from the mechanical extraction of palm oil from the kernel of the oil palm fruit.

With its high availability, cost-effectiveness, and consistent nutritional value, Palm Kernel Expeller has become an essential feed solution for farmers and feed manufacturers seeking efficient, stable, and sustainable livestock nutrition for cattle and cows.

Palm Kernel Expeller Specification

Component Content (% w/w)
  • Crude Protein 14 – 17% for essential growth and muscle development
  • Crude Fiber 14 – 20% supporting healthy rumen function and digestion
  • Crude Fat 8 – 9% providing steady and efficient energy for cattle
  • Moisture less than 10% ensuring longer shelf life and feed stability
  • Ash 3 – 5% offering balanced mineral content for livestock nutrition
Palm Kernel Expeller is formulated to deliver consistent nutritional performance for cattle and cows. Ideal for dairy and beef operations, PKE supports efficient weight gain, improved digestion, and cost-effective feed management.

Benefits of Palm Kernel Expeller (PKE):

a. Balanced Nutritional Profile:

Contains Crude Protein (14–17%), Crude Fiber (14–20%), Crude Fat (8–9%), Moisture (<10%), and Ash (3–5%) to support optimal cattle growth and health.

b. Enhances Rumen Function:

The high fiber content promotes better digestion, improved rumen activity, and stable energy release for both dairy and beef cattle.

c. Cost-Effective Feed Solution:

PKE offers an economical alternative to traditional feed ingredients while maintaining consistent nutritional value.

d. Long Shelf Life & Stable Quality:

Low moisture content ensures better storage stability, reduces spoilage risk, and guarantees consistent feed performance.

e. Sustainable & Abundant Supply:

Produced as a by-product of palm oil extraction, PKE provides a renewable, eco-friendly, and readily available feed option for livestock operations. Palm Kernel Expeller’s balanced composition and reliable performance make it a preferred feed ingredient for farmers seeking efficient, stable, and sustainable nutrition for cattle and cows.
