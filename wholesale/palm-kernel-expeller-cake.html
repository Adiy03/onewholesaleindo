Palm Kernel Cake (PKC), also known as Palm Kernel Expeller (PKE), is a high-value by-product derived from the mechanical pressing of palm kernels to extract vegetable oil.

Despite the different terminology, Palm Kernel Cake (PKC) and Palm Kernel Expeller (PKE) both refer to the same product.

PKC/PKE is widely utilized as animal feed due to its nutritional content that supports dairy and beef cattle, goats, sheep, poultry, and even aquaculture.

In addition, the residual meal can also be used as an organic fertilizer and renewable biomass fuel, making it a versatile product with high added value across various industries.
