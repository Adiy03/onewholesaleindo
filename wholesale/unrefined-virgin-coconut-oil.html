Virgin Coconut Oil (VCO) is a natural, high-quality vegetable oil extracted from fresh, mature coconuts grown in tropical regions, particularly Indonesia.

Produced using cold-pressed or natural fermentation methods, VCO retains its original nutrients, aroma, and purity without the use of chemicals, bleaching, or high-heat processing.

Virgin Coconut Oil is known for its clear appearance, fresh coconut aroma, and high content of medium-chain fatty acids (MCFAs), especially lauric acid, which contributes to its stability and long shelf life.

These characteristics make VCO suitable for a wide range of food-grade, cosmetic, pharmaceutical, and industrial applications, ensuring consistent quality, safety, and performance throughout storage and distribution.

Its renewable and minimally processed nature supports sustainable supply chains and aligns with international food safety and environmental standards.
