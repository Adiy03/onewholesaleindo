Activated Carbon Types for Sale

Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC)

Powdered Activated Carbon is a fine carbon material with particle sizes typically less than 0.18 mm. Due to its high surface area and fast adsorption rate, PAC is ideal for removing organic compounds, chlorine, taste, and odor from water.

It is commonly used in drinking water purification, wastewater treatment, and industrial processes where quick and efficient contaminant removal is required.

Granular Activated Carbon (GAC)

Granular Activated Carbon consists of larger particles, offering excellent filtration performance and longer service life.

GAC is widely applied in continuous flow systems such as industrial water treatment filters and municipal purification systems.

Its larger particle size allows for better flow dynamics, reduced pressure drop, and ease of regeneration, making it suitable for both liquid and gas-phase applications.

Extruded Activated Carbon (EAC)

Extruded Activated Carbon, also known as pelletized activated carbon, is produced by combining powdered carbon with a binding agent and extruding it into cylindrical pellets.

EAC provides high mechanical strength, low dust generation, and uniform density.

It is ideal for air and gas purification, solvent recovery, and industrial water treatment systems where durability and low pressure loss are critical.
