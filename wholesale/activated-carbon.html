Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC) for Sale

Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC) is a highly porous adsorbent material made from natural sources such as coconut shells, wood, or coal.

It is processed into a fine powder with a particle size of less than 0.18 mm, giving it a large surface area and rapid adsorption capability.

These pores significantly increase the total internal surface (up to 1500 m² per gram).

Allowing PAC to quickly trap and hold impurities such as organic compounds, chlorine, and unwanted taste or odor.

Applications:

  • Water Treatment

    Added directly into water treatment systems or mixed with water, then filtered out. Effective for removing odor, taste, chlorine, and harmful contaminants, including PFAS removal.

  • Aquarium & Fish Tank

    Sprinkled or packed into filter sheets to maintain water clarity in reef tanks, ponds, and aquariums, ensuring a healthy ecosystem.

  • Food & Beverage

    Activated carbon is widely used in the food and beverage industry, such as in sugar refining to remove color and odor from raw sugar liquor, in syrup production to ensure clarity and purity

  • Medical Use

    Consumed in powder or tablet form (suspended in water) as an emergency treatment for diarrhea, food poisoning, or poisoning in animals (e.g., dogs with ball belly). Must follow medical/veterinary instructions..

Granular Activated Carbon (GAC)

Larger granules that can be used in carbon filters or bags, and can be regenerated for multiple uses.

Applications & Usage:

  • Water Treatment

    Packed in filter columns or bags for household and industrial installations, ensuring effective purification of drinking water, ponds, and wastewater.

  • Air Purification

    Filled into cartridges or air filters to adsorb odors, gases, and harmful compounds in both residential and industrial settings.

  • Aquarium & Fish Tank

    Placed inside filter media bags or cartridges, highly effective in maintaining water quality in aquariums, decorative ponds, and reef tanks. Needs regular replacement (2–4 weeks).

  • Gold Recovery

    Widely used in mining industries, where gold ions are adsorbed from cyanide solutions using carbon-in-pulp or carbon-in-leach methods.

Pellet / Extruded Activated Carbon (EAC)

Shaped into small cylinders (carbon pellets) with high mechanical strength and resistance to pressure, ideal for gas and air flow applications.

Applications & Usage:

  • CO₂ Adsorption & Carbon Capture

    Used in carbon capture systems for greenhouse gas reduction and eco-friendly technologies.

  • Air & Gas Filtration

    Installed in industrial filters and purifiers for gases, solvents, and air pollution control.

  • Supercapacitors

    Processed into electrodes for modern energy storage due to its high electrical adsorption capacity.

  • Industrial Applications

    Applied in chemical, petrochemical, and factory emission filtration where high mechanical strength is required.
