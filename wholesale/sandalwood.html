Sandalwood for Sale: Indonesia Wholesale Trade

HS Code: 1211XX Medicinal plants, n.e.s.

Sandalwood is an aromatic hardwood from Santalum species.

Valued for its rich fragrance and essential oils. Widely used in perfumes, cosmetics, incense, and traditional medicine

Sandalwood offers a warm, long-lasting scent and soothing properties. Its versatility and cultural importance make sandalwood a highly prized natural resource worldwide.

Our Sandalwood Specifications:

a. Sandalwood Oil

  • Appearance: Pale yellow to golden brown liquid
  • Aroma : Woody
  • Extraction Method: Steam distillation
  • Main Components: α-Santalol 40-45%
  • Capacity : >500 kg oil / month
Renowned for its purity and depth of scent, Sandalwood oil is ideal for use in fine perfumery, luxury cosmetics, aromatherapy, and traditional wellness formulations.

b. Sandalwood Powder

  • Particle size: 80–100 mesh
  • 100% pure, free from additives
Widely used in incense, cosmetics, and skincare formulations. Its smooth texture and therapeutic aroma make it suitable for facial masks, soaps, and fragrance blends that promote relaxation and rejuvenation.

c. Sandalwood Sticks

  • Distinct warm, woody aroma
  • Dense, long-burning wood
  • 100% natural and chemical-free
Perfect for use in incense production, meditation, and religious rituals. Their authentic scent and slow burn make them highly valued for spiritual and aromatic purposes.

d. Sandalwood Chips

Ideal for essential oil distillation, perfumery, and woodcraft applications. Their consistent oil yield and pure aroma profile make them a preferred raw material for traditional extraction and artisan uses.
