Agarwood Oud Oil &
Others Essential Oils


Agarwood oil is an exotic essential oil obtained through steam distillation of agarwood trees which grow naturally in various regions of Indonesia (Sumatra, Kalimantan, Merauke).

The result is an aromatic liquid, ranging from pale yellow to dark brown, with a distinctive agarwood scent—woody, warm, and slightly sweet.

Agar Wood Oud Oil Benefits:

Aromatherapy & Perfumery
The rich, woody, sweet, and soft scent makes it ideal for use in luxury perfumes or space fragrance compositions.

Antimicrobial & Anti-inflammatory
Known for its natural antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties, suitable for topical or diffused applications.

Stress Relief & Relaxation
Commonly used in stress-relief, meditation, and wellness practices due to its calming aroma that enhances mental clarity.

Practical Uses:
  • Massage oil (after dilution)
  • Diffuser or room spray
  • Natural cleanser and air freshener
  • Cosmetic ingredient
  • Flavor enhancer
  • Traditional remedy for respiratory and skin issues

Other uses:
Widely used for meditation, spiritual rituals, and sacred atmosphere creation

Buy Bulk Wholesale Essential Oils from Indonesia

Indonesia has long been recognized as one of the world’s richest sources of botanical and aromatic plants. As global demand for natural and organic wellness products continues to grow, essential oils from Indonesia are increasingly favored by bulk buyers, brand owners, and distributors alike.

Whether you’re in the cosmetic, aromatherapy, fragrance, food, or pharmaceutical industry, sourcing essential oils from Indonesia offers unique advantages that are hard to match.

Benefits of Buying Essential Oils from Indonesia

Diverse Botanical Resources: Indonesia’s tropical climate and fertile volcanic soil nurture a vast range of aromatic plants—from patchouli, clove, nutmeg, ginger, citronella, to sandalwood, vetiver, agarwood, and more. This botanical diversity allows you to access a wide spectrum of essential oils from a single origin.

High-Quality Natural Extraction: Indonesian producers use traditional steam distillation and sustainable harvesting techniques, ensuring oils are pure, unadulterated, and rich in bioactive compounds. Some regions, such as Aceh and Sulawesi, are even known for producing the world’s finest patchouli oil.

Competitive Pricing for Bulk Orders: Indonesia’s integrated agricultural supply chains and strong farming communities make it possible to offer competitive prices without sacrificing quality, especially for bulk and wholesale purchases. Whether you're buying 1 kg or 1 ton, the price-to-quality ratio remains favorable.

Sustainable & Ethical Sourcing: Many Indonesian suppliers work directly with local farmers through cooperatives, ensuring ethical sourcing, fair trade practices, and traceable supply chains—an increasingly critical factor for eco-conscious consumers and global retailers.

Certifications & Global Compliance: Reputable Indonesian essential oil producers often carry certifications such as Halal, Kosher, ISO 9001, FSSC 22000, and GMP, making their products ready for export to markets in the EU, US, Middle East, and Asia.

Custom Blending & Private Label Options: Custom formulations, private labeling, and tailored distillation processes to meet your specific aroma profile, therapeutic goals, or regulatory needs.

Strong Export Infrastructure: With major ports in Surabaya, Jakarta, and Belawan, and reliable air cargo hubs, Indonesia is well-connected to global shipping routes. Most suppliers also partner with international couriers (DHL, FedEx, UPS) to ensure timely delivery worldwide.
