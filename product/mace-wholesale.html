Mace (Organic)

Whole, Broken, Ground, Powder, Spice

Mace (HS Code 0908Xb) is a spice in the form of red or orange lacy threads. With a warm aroma and a flavor milder than nutmeg.

Mace is used as a culinary spice, in traditional medicine, and in perfumes and herbal products.

It is widely used around the world to add a distinctive taste to food and beverages.
